Welcome

I am a NSF Postdoctoral Fellow based at the University of Chicago, Department of Statistics. I am working with Jinho Baik and Peter Miller. I got my PhD from the Indiana University-Purdue University Indianapolis under the advising of Alexander Its in 2019. My main research interests are applications of Riemann-Hilbert problems in probability theory and differential equations.
